About The Bodyweight Gym

Your journey to strength, mobility, and skills starts here

Our Mission

The Bodyweight Gym was created during the 2020 pandemic to provide free, high-quality workout content to everyone, everywhere. We believe that fitness should be accessible to all, regardless of equipment or gym access.

Our team of experienced coaches brings you over 30 hours of professional follow-along workouts, covering strength training, mobility, flexibility, and skill development - all using only your bodyweight.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced athlete, our progressive approach ensures you'll find workouts that challenge and inspire you to reach your fitness goals.
