Our Philosophy

In a fitness world dominated by machines, products, and endless upsells, we believe in something different. Fitness doesn't need to be commercial.

This website exists to share what we've learned about bodyweight fitness. There's no catch. We don't collect emails, don't track you, and don't show ads. Just straightforward fitness education, accessible to everyone.

Your body and gravity are all you need. The process is simple. The long-term application is what challenges us. But you're not alone in this journey.

The Story Behind The Gym

I haven't always been into fitness. I started my adult life as a professional Scientist and for 13 years enjoyed my career.

I spent my 20's racing mountain bikes and rock climbing. My 30's brought about a change where I decided to pursue my lifelong hobby of coaching and sport. I began with CrossFit and for the last 4 years, I've rebuilt myself using Calisthenic bodyweight training methods.

"A few years ago I broke my back. Overnight I went from a ring routine and handstand to not even one pushup. Now I'm back and stronger than ever."

Through this experience, I've learned that the process is easy—it's the long-term application that challenges us. I started The Bodyweight Gym to pursue an ideal: That everyone can develop themselves physically with simple equipment, the right education, and support.

When COVID closed our physical gym, we transformed it into this digital space to ensure that what we learned together wouldn't be lost. Now it's here for anyone who wants to build a stronger, healthier body.
